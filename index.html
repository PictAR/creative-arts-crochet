About Me

Hi! I’m Amanda Arts, the designer behind Creative Arts Crochet in Canada. I create approachable patterns—clean lines, thoughtful texture, and clear instructions—tested by real makers. You’ll find pieces like the Highlands Poncho, Windsong Ruana, and the Lux Solaris earrings on Etsy and Ravelry, with sizes and notes that help you finish with confidence. Thanks for being here!
